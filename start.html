What does 'sobremesa' mean? 🤔
Sobremesa is the Spanish word. It means time spent after lunch/dinner at the table having a pleasant conversation, sipping wine leisurely, telling each other jokes, or sharing pleasant news. Sometimes, but in extremely rare cases, discuss work matters. Although they should not spoil the impression of the meal 🤩
